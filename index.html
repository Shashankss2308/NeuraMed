Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mental health platform and services.

Is my information completely private and secure?

Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and follow strict privacy protocols to ensure your information remains completely confidential and secure.

How do the anonymous group chats work?

Our group chats are completely anonymous. You're assigned a random username and can participate in moderated discussions without revealing your identity.

Are the therapists licensed professionals?

Yes, all our therapists are licensed mental health professionals with verified credentials and extensive experience in their field.

How much does the service cost?

We offer both free and premium services. Basic peer support and assessments are free, while professional therapy sessions have affordable rates.

Can I use this service in crisis situations?

While we provide support, we're not a crisis service. In emergency situations, please contact your local emergency services or crisis hotline.

How does the mental health assessment work?

Our assessment uses evidence-based questions to evaluate your mental wellness and provides personalized insights and recommendations.

Can I switch between different therapists?

Yes, you can request to work with a different therapist if you feel the current match isn't the right fit for your needs.

Is the service available 24/7?

Group chats and some support features are available 24/7. Professional therapist availability varies, but we have therapists across different time zones to provide coverage throughout most hours. Emergency support resources are always accessible.
